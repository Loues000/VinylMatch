About VinylMatch

Bridging your digital playlists with the world of physical records

What is VinylMatch?

VinylMatch pairs Spotify tracks with Discogs releases, making it easy for collectors, DJs, and vinyl enthusiasts to discover the perfect pressing for their favourite songs. Simply paste a Spotify playlist link and let VinylMatch find matching vinyl releases automatically.

Key Features

  • Spotify Integration — OAuth login with automatic token refresh keeps you connected.
  • Smart Matching — Progressive Discogs search that prefers barcode matches when available.
  • Shop Links — Quick shortcuts to HHV, JPC, Amazon and custom vinyl shops for every matched release.
  • Playlist Memory — Recent playlist history so you can jump back into your latest searches.
  • Curation Tools — Manual matching for edge cases where automatic matching falls short.
