About VinylMatch

Connect digital playlists with physical releases

What is VinylMatch?

VinylMatch bridges your digital playlists and the physical record world. Paste any Spotify playlist link and VinylMatch instantly cross-references every track against Discogs — surfacing the right pressings and direct vendor search links, all in one pass.

Key Features

  • Spotify integration — Seamless OAuth login with persistent session support.
  • Smart matching — Progressive Discogs search with intelligent fallbacks to maximize hit rate.
  • Vendor links — One-click search across HHV, JPC, Amazon, and custom vendors.
  • Recent playlists — Pick up right where you left off with instant access to your last sessions.
  • Curation tools — Fine-tune results manually for obscure or mismatched releases.
