About VinylMatch

VinylMatch bridges your digital playlists with the world of physical records by pairing Spotify tracks with Discogs releases. It is built for collectors, DJs and anyone who wants to discover the perfect pressing for their favourite songs.

Key features

  • Spotify OAuth login with automatic token refresh.
  • Progressive Discogs search that prefers barcode matches when available.
  • Quick shortcuts to external vinyl shops for every matched release.
  • Recent playlist memory so you can jump back into your latest searches.
